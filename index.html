About the Initiative

According to global reports, 15% of working-age adults live with a mental health disorder. Employment conditions play a major role in mental well-being, with structured work environments promoting stability and poor conditions increasing mental health risks.

Informal workers in India face specific challenges, such as financial instability, lack of healthcare, and limited mental health resources. Our initiative seeks to raise awareness and encourage support for improved mental health services and resources for informal workers.

    Key Challenges

    Informal workers experience challenges that impact their mental health and well-being:

    • Financial instability and lack of income security
    • Limited access to healthcare and mental health services
    • Poor working conditions and lack of social protection
    • Social stigma and isolation

    Recommended Actions

    To support the mental health needs of informal workers, we recommend the following actions:

    • Establish social welfare programs and financial support
    • Expand access to affordable and comprehensive mental health services
    • Promote secure employment and safe working conditions
    • Increase awareness to reduce stigma around mental health
